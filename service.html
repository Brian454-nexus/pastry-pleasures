Welcome to Pastry Pleasures Baking School

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned baker, our classes are designed to elevate your skills and ignite your passion for baking. Explore our masterclasses, learn new techniques, and discover our delicious range of cakes, cupcakes, and pastries!

Fondant Masterclass

  • Duration: 12hrs (4 days)
  • Cost: Ksh. 30,000
  • Time: 9:00AM–11:30AM or 1:00PM–3:30PM
  • Days: Monday–Thursday

This class is for bakers who want to master fondant for weddings and large cakes. Suitable for ages 13+ with basic cake decorating knowledge.

  • Cake leveling, trimming, layering
  • Ganache & fondant covering
  • Stacking, dowelling, fixing mistakes
  • Consultations, contracts, costing
Note: 70% payment required for booking. All equipment & ingredients provided. Non-refundable. Max 3 students per class.
