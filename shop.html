Design Your Dream Cake or Pastry With Us

How to Place a Custom Order:
  • Fill out the form below with as much detail as possible.
  • For design inspiration, you can upload an image of a cake you like.
  • Once we receive your request, we will contact you within 24 hours to confirm the details and provide a price quote.
  • A 50% deposit is required to confirm your order. The balance is due upon pickup or delivery.
